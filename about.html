Flexible Delivery Model

Onsite Services Model

iGen consultants deployed ar client side working under the customer's project management structure

100% Offshore Services Model

iGen team from our offshore site carrying out the software engineering work

Client representative managing the project coordination on client's side and interacting with iGen's project manager

Blended Onsite - Offshore Model

Small team of iGen consultants working onsite for coordination, business analysis, architecture, integration and deployment activities. The bulk of the team offshore carrying out architecture, design, development and testing activities

Project/work packets delivered on a fixed price basis, typically

Bulk of maintenance and support work being done from the offshore site

Dedicated Offshore Development Center (ODC)

For customer's long term strategic advantage, iGen can set up a dedicated ODC. Suited for clients wishing to execute multiple IT AD/AM/Testing programs on a long term basis [3-5 years planning horizon]

Dedicated manpower, technologies, and domain expertise

Dedicated hardware and software infrastructure, dedicated high-speed communication equipment, and bandwidth

COE Model (Center of Excellence Model)

Best suited when customers needs support on a specific product/technology/platform/business area and want to continuous enrichment on this area

Examples include Integration CoE, BI/Reporting CoE, etc
